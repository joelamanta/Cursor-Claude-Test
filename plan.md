# Continu.photos — Portfolio Website Plan

## Overview
A minimal brutalist portfolio for Continu.photos, a designer and photographer.
Built with plain HTML, CSS, and JS — no frameworks.

---

## Design Direction: Minimal Brutalism

- Raw, honest layout — no rounded corners, no shadows, no gradients
- Strong typographic hierarchy (bold weights, large sizes, uppercase labels)
- Monochrome base (black, white, off-white) with one accent (TBD — red, yellow, or raw paper tone)
- Grid-based, asymmetric layouts
- Borders used as structure, not decoration
- Cursor: custom or crosshair
- Fonts: a brutalist-friendly pairing — e.g., Space Grotesk or IBM Plex Mono + a heavy sans

---

## Pages / Sections (Single Page)

### 1. Hero / Header
- Site name: CONTINU.PHOTOS
- Tagline: e.g., "Designer. Photographer."
- Navigation: minimal — ABOUT / PROJECTS / CONTACT (inline, top right)

### 2. About
- Short bio paragraph
- What you do, your approach, your eye
- Optional: one strong portrait or abstract image

### 3. Projects
- Grid of project cards (image + title + category label)
- Filter or just curated selection (photography / design)
- Clicking a card: lightbox or link to external

### 4. Contact
- Email address (plain text, large)
- Social links (Instagram, Behance, etc.)
- Optional: simple contact form

### 5. Footer
- Copyright line
- Back-to-top
